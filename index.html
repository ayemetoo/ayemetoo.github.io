Introduction

Hello, my name is Amy, and I am a 2nd year Computer Science and Engineering student at UCLA (as you have probably already noticed). I love cats, sunsets, and rich purple hues. This here is my attempt at making a website!


The quote on the cover of this website changes each time you load the page (unless you're using Internet Explorer).
